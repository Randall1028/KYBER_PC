# KYBER v0.87.0 (beta)

A big upgrade to how your droid *feels*: a ground-up rework of the emotion engine so its reactions actually match what you say.

## Smarter emotional reactions
- Your droid now reads the **meaning** of what you say instead of matching keywords, so it reacts naturally to praise, threats, bad news, teasing, and everything in between — with far fewer flat "curious/confused" responses.
- **It has empathy now.** Talk about something sad — a loss, being left behind, even another droid getting scrapped — and it responds with sadness instead of a shrug.

## Knows its friends and enemies (celebrity droids)
- R2-D2, BB-8, Chopper, and BD-1 now react true to character: delight at allies (Luke, Leia, Chewbacca), and wariness, fear, or anger at enemies (Darth Vader, Kylo Ren, the Empire, stormtroopers) — even when a name only comes up in passing.
- Beat the bad guy and it celebrates. An enemy is a threat, but a *defeated* enemy is a win.

## Emotional momentum
- Feelings now carry for a beat — a scared droid stays a little on edge — but a clear new feeling always takes over instantly. Scare it, then reassure it, and it's happy right away, not stuck.

## Custom droids stay yours
- Build your own droid and it reacts purely by its personality and the situation, with no forced good-guy lean. A cold, bold, or villainous build finally feels that way.

## Also in this release
- **Dual-world savvy:** real-world references (Disney World, pizza, music) no longer confuse it.
- **Com Uplink dock** now updates the droid model and name the instant you change and save them — no restart needed.
- Snappier, more reliable reactions; a genuine "confused" mood no longer trips the tray "glitched" indicator; cleaner Ollama shutdown on quit; and fixes to voice-command over-triggering.

---
*Still in beta — expect a few rough edges, and please keep the bug reports coming.*
